The analysis of the uptake of electric vehicles in Australia in relation to national electricity generation/consumption as well as the factors that might be affecting this uptake including global crude oil prices, lithium prices and global average temperature increase.

And through machine learning methods predict and forecast the future trends of this electric vehicle uptake and the potential factors affecting this uptake including global crude oil prices, lithium prices and global average temperature increase.

The aim of this final project was to analyse the current trends in the electric vehicle uptake and national electricity generation and consumption. To accomplish this analysis, we obtained datasets from various sources. After assembling the datasets, we used Pandas and Matplotlib to plot current electricity generation by fuel type and by state, electric vehicle sales, lithium prices, global crude oil prices and global average temperature increase, etc.

In terms of the machine learning aspect, we used Prophet library to generate time series model to perform our forecasts and prediction on the various aspect we want to analyse including lithium prices, electric vehicle sales forecast, global crude oil prices forecast, global temperature increase forecast etc.
